Trying to make music with friends online is painful. You email MP3s, talk on Skype, and share files on Dropbox; can't we do better than cobbling all these things together? JamHive solves this with a place where you and your bandmates can record, edit, and share all your music online.

Easily record audio in your web browser, share tracks with your bandmates, and edit online. Keep track of changes to your songs as well as get people's comments.

FAQ

When will Jamhive be available?
We expect to have a beta ready in October.
How much will Jamhive cost?
It will be free for you to post tracks that are public. It will cost $5/month for a premium account where you get an unlimited number of private tracks.
